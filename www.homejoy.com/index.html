How it Works

Book your cleaning and confirm your appointment. It's that easy!

1Book Your Cleaning

Just tell us your location, preferred time, and how many hours you'd like to book a Homejoy Cleaner. If you're not sure how long the cleaning job should take don't worry, we will help you estimate.

2Appointment Confirmation

We will contact you to confirm your booking and clarify any special instructions you may have. When we find the perfect Homejoy Cleaner for you, we will send you their profile (including a photo) so you can get to know them before they even show up.

3Cleaning Completed!

Your Homejoy Cleaner will arrive at the scheduled time and location. Upon completion, your credit card will be charged for the owed amount. We ask that you leave a review for your Homejoy Cleaner to help them continue improving their services!

About Homejoy

Cleaning Services For Everyone

At Homejoy we want to make cleaning services available for a broad audience, rather than a luxury for only the rich.

We make managing your cleaning service convenient: you can book, cancel, and reschedule cleanings directly on the Homejoy website via your computer or smartphone.

Each Homejoy Cleaner gets a background check. We interview and test each cleaner in-person. Furthermore, all cleans are bonded and insured and frequently checked for quality. This assures you of high-quality service.

Just as your happiness is a priority for us, we also want to make sure that all Homejoy Cleaners are well-treated.

Based in San Francisco, California we are expanding to more cities quickly. Homejoy Cleaners are currently available in New York City, San Francisco, Los Angeles, San Diego, San Jose and Seattle.
